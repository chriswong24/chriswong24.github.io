Who?

I grew up as the youngest of three brothers in Mamaroneck, NY. In 2012, I began to attend University of Rochester as a member of the Class of 2016. I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Business.

I love the sport of Ultimate with an undying passion. In the past, I have been the captain of my high school team and went to Youth Club Championships in Minnesota. Currently, I play for University of Rochester men's ultimate team - the Piggies.

I'm always up for new adventures to add to my story. Get in touch, and lets make history together.
